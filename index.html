Leander Capuozzo Photographer - Director - Producer New York, New York

Leander Capuozzo is a New York-based photographer, director, and producer working across fashion, music, art, and independent media. His work focuses on people, clothing, performance, and the culture around them.

In addition to producing editorial, video, and cultural projects, he has produced full-scale fashion shows and live runway presentations, overseeing creative production, casting, staging, and show execution.

He has contributed photography, direction, and production to editorial stories, runway coverage, music videos, campaigns, and cultural projects for artists and brands including ANNA BOLINA, Vogue, Interview, i-D, PAPER, Hypebeast, Cultured, and Office Magazine.
